Our Mission

We live in an age of information, but who has access to it? Just a select few? HeronAI is on a mission to change that. We're not here to just make a product; we're here to make a difference, to put a dent in the universe. HeronAI is about empowering people, small businesses, and non-profits to embrace the future — a future where data is not the realm of just tech giants and elite companies but accessible and useful for everyon

HeronAI is revolutionizing the way nonprofits and companies are measuring impact and driving revenue. “55% to 80% of the data that a business stores [is] dark.” meaning that even though businesses spend money collecting data it often isn’t used to help them drive profit and ultimately impact their organization’s as a whole.

The problem in these companies is all the software that is available requires specialized training, even the ones with automated features, and there isn’t one platform for centralizing data specific for these industries making analysis tedious time consuming and often duplicating effort across platforms. If they have a solution in place it’s a clunky solution that isn’t centralized or automated and if a team member leaves the learning curve is so steep it can take months to for a new team member to catch up.

We believe in empowering businesses to think differently about their data. In a world where data is ubiquitous, understanding it shouldn't be exclusive to a select few. That's why we're committed to equalizing data analytics, and breaking down barriers and complexities to make insights accessible for everyone.

Data Freedom for everyone

Data analytics today is like the early days of computing, reserved for the elite. HeronAI is on a mission to change that, providing the 'underdog' access to insights usually reserved for tech giants. With our AI-driven platform, we're inviting everyone to embrace a future where data is personal, accessible, and transformative.

We envision a future where every team member, from marketing or sales to C-suites, can make informed decisions powered by their own data, fueling innovation, growth, and success. Our mission is to challenge the status quo, to transform how organizations interact with data, creating a world where data-driven decision-making is not the exception, but the norm.
