HeronAI

We're the first AI-powered, fully automated data analytics platform for nonprofits. Merging advanced technology with user-friendliness and affordability, we centralize data, automate processing, and craft queriable reports. We empower nonprofits to amplify their impact, drive revenue, and uncover invaluable insights.

We believe in the power of accessible data to drive significant improvements across all fields. Our purpose is to make data analytics simple and easy for everyone, not just data scientists, because an informed society can transform the world.

HeronAI was founded to create a future where every team member, from marketing or sales to C-suites, can make informed decisions powered by their own data, fueling innovation, growth, and success. Our mission is to challenge the status quo, to transform how organizations interact with data, creating a world where data-driven decision-making is not the exception, but the norm.
